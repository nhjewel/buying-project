Fabric Kniting

We have some Fabric knitting partners in Bangladesh. Our Partnar Maruf Knitting Factory serves as a fully vertical knit fabric and apparel manufacturing setup in Bangladesh (yarn-final product) producing approx. 3 million units per month and 500,000 KGs of knit fabric per month for various retailers across Europe and North America. Our vertical integration assists us in providing competitive prices, robust development services, and quick turnarounds to achieve significant economic of scale for our customers.

Printing

Secura Bangladesh Limited (SBL) is a producer of high quality printed products and printing solutions. SBL specializes in designing, processing and printing of all kinds of print items. They have stringent quality control procedures & so, we ensure minimum job turnaround time at most competitive rates.

Embroidery

We are associated with some large scale composite garment factories, print and embroidery factories including washing plant in Bangladesh. We have own sourcing of fabrics and accessories within the country as well as in Bangladesh, China.
